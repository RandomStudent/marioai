   -srf & Set receptive field (grid) visible & $<$on$|$off$>$ Default: off & {\texttt Main -srf on} \\
   \hline
   -mm & Mario Mode. 0 -- small,1 - large, 2 - large, fire ability is available & $<$0$|$1$|$2$>$. Default: 2 & {\texttt Main -mm 1} \\
